Database Integration Plan

User Feedback and Reviews:

  • Users will have the ability to submit reviews and ratings for bikes, accessories, and biking trails.
  • Feedback will be stored in the database and displayed alongside product listings, providing valuable insights for potential buyers.

Exclusive Deals:

  • PedalPulse will collaborate with leading bike manufacturers and retailers to offer exclusive deals and discounts to platform users.
  • Deals and promotions will be dynamically updated based on user preferences and browsing history, enhancing the shopping experience.

Upcoming Releases and News:

  • The platform will feature a dedicated section for news and updates, keeping users informed about upcoming bike releases, industry events, and biking-related news.
  • Integration with RSS feeds or APIs from reputable sources will ensure timely and relevant content delivery to users.
